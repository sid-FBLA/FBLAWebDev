About

Total Row was created by Husband and Wife team, Elliott and Lauren Smith. After a series of injuries from running, weight lifting, and skiing sidelined Elliott, the two knew there had to be a better way to safely exercise. They also dreaded the inconvenience and overcrowding of big box gyms, combined with the staleness of doing the same workout each day. Many times, rather than something to look forward to, a workout became a chore instead. At Total Row, you will experience a complete fitness program that is uncommon to boutique fitness. Rather than looking at only a single class experience, we look at your ENTIRE fitness journey from start to finish.
